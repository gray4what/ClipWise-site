ClipWise — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Summary: ClipWise does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All data is stored locally in your browser. The extension makes zero network requests.

Data Collection

ClipWise does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is collected
  • No analytics or telemetry data is collected
  • No cookies are used
  • No third-party tracking scripts are included

Data Storage

All data you create with ClipWise (clips, projects, tags, ratings, and notes) is stored exclusively in your browser's local IndexedDB database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synced to any server
  • Is not accessible to the extension developer or any third party
  • Can be exported by you at any time as a JSON backup file
  • Can be deleted by you at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data

Network Requests

ClipWise makes zero network requests. The extension operates entirely offline after installation. No data is ever transmitted from your browser to any external server.

Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions required to function:

  • Side Panel — to display the notebook interface
  • Content Scripts — to inject clip buttons on ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini pages and read conversation content when you click a clip button
  • Storage — to persist your data locally in IndexedDB

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. The core commitment — no data collection, no network requests, local-only storage — will not change.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at clipwise.ext@gmail.com.
